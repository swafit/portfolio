Gardens on the Bolfracks Estate near Aberfeldy – Perthshire, Scotland

Obair is Gaelic for work. It is related to the Latin opus which gives us words like opera and operate. So you could say that this website is m’obair – my work.


Also, my McDonald ancestors came from Obair-pheallaidh, known in English as Aberfeldy. In this case, obair is simply the Gaelic form of the Pictish word aber, meaning the mouth of a stream. Pheallaidh comes from Peallach, the name of a water sprite.
