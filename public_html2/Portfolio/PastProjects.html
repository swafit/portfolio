Past Projects

The skills used on Success Airlines were image maps, floats, highlights and margins. An image map was used to make the map link to different pages representing the sections of the airport. Margins made it easier to read and the floats moved the images to the left or right. The float also allowed for the two column layout.

On Bean's Beans, I used color gradients, text decorations such as underlines and shadows.

On Toronto Islands Ferry, I used tables to display the various prices. I used the table head to make a category for monthly and one for annually.

The skills used on JavaJam Coffee House were floats, sections and asides for the ads and for the navbar. Used tables for the menu. Added audio to two pages and created 2 forms. One for the gear on sale and one for job hirings.
